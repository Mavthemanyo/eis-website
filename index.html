01 — About

Markets, taught by students,
for students.

We run weekly meetings that include market news, company analyses, open floor stock pitches, competition preparation, and slideshow presentation — open to all. Members who want to get more involved can join our competition teams and create pitches to present to the society or to competition committees.

The society is completely member-managed. Coheads are elected each spring, meeting topics are member-proposed, and the student-run investment simulator is accessible to all — no investing knowledge required.

02 — Opportunities

Six ways to get involved.

Come for one meeting or come for all — every meeting is open to every member, every term.

01

Stock Pitches

Individuals or small teams research a company, build a thesis, and pitch it live to the club.

02

Speakers

Alumni or Faculty in finance and investing join us throughout the year for conversations about markets, careers, and adivce worth absorbing.

03

Trading Simulation

A digital trading competition open to all members hosted each term, with a leaderboard and a small prize for the individual with the most capital at the end of the term.

04

Competitions

We send teams to prestigious investment and pitch competitions each year.
